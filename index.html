About

Thanks for dropping in to my corner of utopia. I started Borja Design as a personal outlet for creative growth. I work freelance in photography, graphic design and front end development. My goal is creating visual concepts in graphic designs and web development for local businesses around Los Angeles. As you grow, I grow, too.

Moreover, having this website has kept me in touch with my community and adds a wonderful way of expressing my vision through visual art and web design. For more about me click below.
